Innovation

Aerodynamics Reimagined.

Closed-loop Stability

Onboard IMU and GPS sensing to continuously adjust thrust and control surfaces.

Ventral Keel Fin

Underbody stabilizing surface added to increase yaw damping and directional stability.

Integrated Payload Bay

Internal fuselage cabin that keeps mass near the center of gravity and protects cargo.

Wing-area Optimization

Balanced wing geometry to strengthen ground-effect without adding unnecessary drag.
